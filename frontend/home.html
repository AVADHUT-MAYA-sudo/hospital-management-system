Welcome to Hospital Management System

Our Hospital Management System is a digital platform designed to simplify hospital administration, patient registration, clinic management, and subscription handling. It provides a seamless experience for patients, clinics, administrators, and super admins through an organized and secure system.

Patients can quickly register their clinic details, administrators can review applications, approve registrations, manage clinic subscriptions, and maintain operational transparency. The platform helps reduce paperwork, improves workflow efficiency, and supports better healthcare service management.
